A Pirate’s life.
You are a pirate captain in the year 1700, you have a population of pirates under your control. Goal: You must grow your pirate armada over time to one day conquer the seas and fight the final boss. Obstacles: Every year that passes there is a chance that something will happen to your armada, such as: disease, kraken, and storms. Along the way players will deal with losing battles due to facing them prematurely, varying prices and values of commodities, as well as fluctuating fish and treasure yields. Rules: ● At any point of the game you may challenge the final boss, if you lose you must restart. If you win you will become the pirate lord and you will be compared against other pirate legends via leaderboards. ● You may also challenge the royal navy at any time with your total pirate armada, with each battle getting progressively harder, if you win you gain the spoils of the battle such as: captured navy men, fish, and doubloons, if you lose you lose varying amounts of pirates, boats, cannons and fish. ● At the start of the game you begin with 5 boats with 5 cannons on each, 50 pirates, 150 fish, and 25 doubloons. ● You may click a button to advance one year in which you must feed your pirates fish to keep them alive, if you malnourished them, some or many may die. Each pirate requires 5 fish annually to survive, a random amount of pirates will join your armada each year. ● Pirates can be assigned to go fishing(1 pirate yields 1-3 fish annually), go treasure hunting(5 pirates minimum and yields 5-10 doubloons annually),build boats(10 pirates minimum and yields 1 boat annually) ● Each boat is allowed 5 cannons and 10 pirates maximum for battles. The more cannons you have, the more damage you will do during battles leading to an increased chance of victory. ● There is a market in which you can buy and sell boats, cannons, and fish, prices and values will slightly vary randomly each year. ● If more than 50% of your pirates die from starvation, you may be thrown overboard and forced to restart.
